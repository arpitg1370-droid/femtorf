Company Overview

Femtorf is a cutting-edge Indian company, headquartered in Ahmedabad, specializing in advanced microelectronics assembly and high-reliability packaging solutions for mission-critical applications. We support strategic sectors such as space, aerospace, defence, and high-performance computing, with a strong emphasis on indigenous capability and system reliability.

Core Competencies

  • Flip-chip & wire bonding
  • Die attach & SMT
  • Hybrid integration

Advanced Packaging

  • Hermetic & non-hermetic options
  • Multi-chip modules (MCM)
  • Ceramic/organic substrates

Testing & Production

  • Environmental & electrical reliability tests
  • Thermal cycling, vibration, burn-in
  • Rapid prototyping — scalable production

Clients & Partnerships

  • ISRO, DRDO, HAL, Defence PSUs
  • Public sector R&D collaboration

Our Services

Microelectronics Assembly

Advanced flip-chip bonding, wire bonding, die attach, and surface mount technology to assure electrical/mechanical reliability for mission-critical components.

Advanced Packaging Solutions

Hermetic and non-hermetic packaging, multi-chip modules (MCM), ceramic/organic substrates customized for harsh aerospace and defense environments.

Testing & Quality Assurance

Comprehensive thermal cycling, vibration, burn-in, and X-ray inspection processes, fully aligned with ISO 9001, AS9100, MIL-STD standards.

Rapid Prototyping & Production

Low and medium-volume production runs, custom design for rapid proof-of-concept and new product development.
